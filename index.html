About Ainia Dental

As a general family oriented dental clinic, we provide a high quality of dental care in a comfortable, relaxing and professional environment for affordable fees. We have a patient focused approach and use preventative and restorative dentistry to optimize and promote your overall oral health. By combining the latest evidence-based knowledge with a dedication to technical mastery, the result is complete and lasting dentistry for our patients.

Our mission is to establish long-term relationships with our patients with professional service through personalized, honest and informed care. We aim to help our community achieve excellent oral healthcare through experienced and cost efficient dental services.
